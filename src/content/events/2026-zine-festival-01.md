# English

## ■ Overview

I am planning to participate in the **ZINE Festival**.

At the event, visitors will be able to browse and read the works produced through **PochomLab** in a physical format.

The following works are planned to be presented:

- **Pochomkin Egaki-sho** (Chapter 1–4)
- **Let's Create Egaki-sho! ZINE**
  - Creation Guide
  - Character Making Guide
  - Publishing Guide

These ZINEs document the creative process and production workflow behind the project.

---

## ■ Exhibition

The following publications will be available at the booth:

- **Pochomkin Egaki-sho** – Chapter 1 to Chapter 4  
- **Egaki-sho Creation ZINE**
  - Creation
  - Character Making
  - Publishing

The booth will also introduce the creative workflow using generative AI and the development logs behind the project.

---

## ■ About the Event

The **ZINE Festival** is an event focused on independent publications such as ZINEs, art books, and small press works.

Creators gather to share and present their independently produced works.

For details about the event, please visit the official page:
